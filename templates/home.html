About This Edition

MTDify Self-Hosted Edition is designed for users who prefer owning their own data and keeping it fully under their control. Everything is processed directly on your own hosting server (or I can host it for you) using a SQLite database.

This edition is ideal for solo developers, privacy-focused users, and anyone who wants a straightforward bookkeeping tool without a subscription.
